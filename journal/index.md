# Journal

## Recent Entry

### The Work of Noticing

Noticing is a kind of care. It asks for patience, for slowness, for the willingness to look twice at the ordinary things that usually pass by without comment. In that attention, a small moment can become a record of how a life is being lived.

This journal is still growing, and each entry is meant to feel like a small opening rather than a finished statement.
