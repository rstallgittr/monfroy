## Recent on My journey
Normal life as in the boring one that is happening right now. Not boring, but lacking flow and experience and emotion. So yes, boring. Because yes I am insecure about a few things, and they will cripple me if I let them. I will not let them. I will challenge them head on. Those cannot be challenged with all my effort in this current job and world I find myself in. I must change worlds in order to best attack my weaknesses, so that I may build them into strengths. The goal is to be a pure human. No insecurities, no ego (ego is ok in small doses), strength that people can lean on, experience that gives perspective. Love, emotion, loss, grief, growth. Meaningful contribution to the lives of my local and global. I’m tired of “I don’t know what is mine to do” I shall go and find that which is mine to do. That which is mine to see and feel. I have the base: the intense curiosity, the capacity for nuance, the tolerance, the Power, the philosophical reason, the technical expertise, the skill in my hands, the hearing in my ears. I am just missing the truly unshakeable strength found in purpose. My Ikigai, so to speak. I am a stone pillar. I know that. They know that. Just a few stops on the journey away from beginning to grow into that immense frame granted to me by the earth.

### The Work of Noticing

Noticing is a kind of care. It asks for patience, for slowness, for the willingness to look twice at the ordinary things that usually pass by without comment. In that attention, a small moment can become a record of how a life is being lived.

This journal is still growing, and each entry is meant to feel like a small opening rather than a finished statement.
